About Us

TT Locks & hardwares is a company that specializes in the manufacturing and distribution of high-quality locks and hardware products. We have been in the industry for over 35 years and have established a strong reputation for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our products are designed and manufactured using the latest technology and materials, ensuring that they are durable, reliable, and easy to use. We offer a wide range of locks and hardware products, including deadbolts, door handles, knobs, and more, to meet the needs of residential, commercial, and industrial customers.

At TT Locks & hardwares, we believe in providing exceptional customer service and support. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to helping our customers find the perfect lock and hardware solution for their needs. We offer competitive pricing, fast shipping, and easy returns, ensuring that our customers have a positive experience with us.

We are committed to sustainability and responsible manufacturing practices. We use eco-friendly materials and processes whenever possible, and we work closely with suppliers to ensure that our products are produced in an environmentally responsible manner.

Thank you for choosing TT Locks & hardwares. We look forward to serving you and providing you with the best possible products and service.
